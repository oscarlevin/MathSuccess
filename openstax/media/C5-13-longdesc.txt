The map shows the &#8220;Order of Napoleon&#8217;s Battle of Waterloo&#8221; marking events that took place between 15 June and 18 June. The major events are the French movement, Anglo-Dutch movement (advance/retreat), and the Prussian movement (advance/retreat). It also marks the Anglo-Dutch line of communication, Prussian line of communication, and the army boundary. Ligny (16 June), Quatre-Bras (16 June), and Wavre (18 June) are marked as French victories while Waterloo (18 June) is marked as Allied victory. The map also marks different headquarters, cavalry (corps/division), French Imperial Guard, and Corps.
